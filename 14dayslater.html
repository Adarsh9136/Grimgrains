A record of what Devine & I eat, everyday, for a period of 2 weeks. All recipes are portioned for 2 people, except breakfasts (we don't eat the same thing).

Tuesday

Breakfast

  • 1 cup quick oats (plain)
  • 1/2 cup fortified soy milk
  • 1 tsp ground flax seeds
  • 1/3 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup wholegrain O's cereal
  • 1/4 cup granola (homemade)
  • 1 cup fortified soy milk
  • 1/4 cup blueberries

Lunch

  • 4 Veggie patties
  • 2 Black olive ciabatta
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 pickle
  • 1/2 cup sauerkraut
  • 2 apple

Bring a pan to high heat, add some vegetable oil and 1/2 chopped yellow onion, cook until browned, then add 2 minced garlic cloves. Cook for 1 minute, add 1 grated carrot, 1 grated potato and 1/4 grated block of firm tofu. Cook for 5 minutes, add some ground cumin, black pepper and salt, and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Transfer veggies to bowl, process into a paste with an immersion blender or food processor, add 1/2 cup of powdered oats (quick oats that are ground into a powder). Shape into patties, and cook into a pan with a bit of oil until browned on both sides. Makes about 6 patties.

Dinner

  • 3 slices slices of millet tourtiere
  • 4 blocks of sea salt black chocolate

Wednesday

Breakfast

  • 1 cup quick oats (plain)
  • 250ml fortified soy milk
  • 5ml ground flax seeds
  • 1/3 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup wholegrain O's cereal
  • 1/4 cup granola (homemade)
  • 250ml fortified soy milk
  • 1/4 cup blueberries

Lunch

Warm lentil salad:

  • 400ml cooked brown lentils (140ml dry)
  • 15 ml soy sauce
  • 15 ml maple syrup (sweetener)
  • 15 ml apple cider vinegar
  • 5 ml dijon mustard

Cook lentils with vegetable broth (for better flavor). Mix the sauce ingredients together in a bowl, pour over lentils.

Roasted potatoes and brussel sprouts:

  • 225g brussel sprouts (of whatever green veggie you have)
  • Sweet potatoes
  • 15ml of olive oil
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 5ml balsamic vinegar
  • 5ml brown sugar

Heat over to 220˚C (425°F). Cut stem and remove outer leaves from brussel sprouts, cut in half. Cut sweet potatoes into thin wedges. In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, balsamic vinegar and brown sugar, mix well. Add cut sweet potatoes and brussel sprouts, mix until well coated. Spread the veggies on a baking tray, and bake for 30 minutes.

Mix all the ingredients together, and season with salt and pepper.
