About

We started Grimgrains to teach ourselves how to cook. This blog, which now doubles as a travel diary, helps measure our progress, while giving us another creative output. We cook and travel, and adapt to the local produce whenever possible. All of the recipes we make are plant-based, and tree-nut free.

We are Rekka Bellum, an illustrator, and Devine Lu Linvega, an artist/musician, living on a small sailboat somewhere on the shores of the Pacific Ocean. Hundredrabbits is the name of our floating studio, we make games, tools and delicious recipes as we travel.

This website showcases our favourite creations, along with hand-drawn illustrations for each ingredient. You can contact us on our twitter @grimgrains or via Mastodon
