chickpea salad

  • For veganaise, see the recipe.
  • Cook 3/4 cup of dried chickpeas (see instructions), or use 1 can (15oz).
  • Mix the cooked chickpeas with 3-4 tbsp of venagaise, 1 tbsp dijon mustard, 1/2 tsp bull kelp powder, 2 stalks of chopped scallions and 1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper powder.
  • Season with black pepper and shichimi togarashi, and serve over toasted bread.
