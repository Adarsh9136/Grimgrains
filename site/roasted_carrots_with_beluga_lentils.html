roasted carrots with beluga lentils

carrots

  • Preheat oven to 400F
  • Wash 8 organic heirloom carrots but do not peel them. Dry the carrots, and then toss them with 1 tbsp of olive oil and 1 tsp of thyme.
  • Lay on a baking sheet and sprinkle with a dash of salt and some black pepper. Bake for 25 minutes, making sure to flip the carrots halfway through.

sauce

  • Mix 2 tbsp of sriracha, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 1/4 cup soy sauce and 2 tbsp Japanese rice vinegar in a small bowl.

lentils

  • Wash 1/2 cup of beluga lentils. Put 375 ml of vegetable bouillon in a pot and add the lentils, bring to a boil.
