pan fried breadfruit

breadfruit

  • Preheat oven to 425F.
  • Brush outside of breadfruit with olive oil.
  • Wrap in aluminum foil, and bake for 1 hour.
  • To check if it is ready, poke a knife through. If it is soft, it is ready.
  • Remove foil, and peel skin away. Then, cut in half and scoop out the seed in the middle.
  • Cut into wedges, and saut√©e in a pan with a bit of oil. Season with some salt and pepper.
  • Cook both sides until crispy and golden.

toppings

  • Mix 4 tbsp soy sauce and 2 tsp of sugar in a pan, stir, and cook on medium-heat for a few minutes. Add button mushrooms, and stir to coat them with the sauce. Serve over breadfruit wedges.
