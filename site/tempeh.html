tempeh

Tempeh is a soy product, made from fermented soy beans. Tempeh is a source of protein, Omega-3's and zinc, and has a firm texture and earthy flavor.Tempeh is made by controlled fermentation that binds soybeans into a 'paddy'. The fungus Rhizopus Oligosporus is used as a starter to make tempeh. Fermented soyfoods like tempeh increased iron absorption.
