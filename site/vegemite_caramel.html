vegemite caramel

1/2 pint — 20 minutes

Vegemite is very salty, and i thought would pair well with something sweet.

I used deglet noor dates for this recipe (because it's what I had). Feel free to use medjool dates instead. Medjool dates are great, but you don't have to use them. They're expensive, and their cheaper counterpart works just fine.

caramel

deglet noor dates 1 cup
vegemite 1 1/2 tsp
lemon juice 1 tsp
soy milk 4 tbsp
vanilla extract 1 tsp
  • Soak 1 cup of deglet noor dates in boiling water for 5-10 minutes. Drain.
  • Mix dates with the following ingredients - 1 1/2 tsp of vegemite, 1 tsp of lemon juice, 4 tbsp of soy milk and 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  • Blend to a smooth purée using a hand mixer or food processor. Serve as a dip for apple slices!
